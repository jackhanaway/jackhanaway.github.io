My name is Jack Hanaway, and baseball has been a constant throughout my life. My experience as a player, coach, and overall student of the game has led me to seek how understand baseball through data, and create actionable insights for real results.

I created this site to organize and share the analytical work I have done across collegiate and professional baseball environments, and to push myself to communicate findings clearly, both in writing and in the tools I build.

I am currently a senior at the University of Tennessee, where I am finishing a Bachelor's of Science in Sport Management with a Minor in Statistics. I will be pursuing a Master of Science in Business Analytics beginning in the summer of 2026. My technical work spans R, R Shiny, and Python, and I have hands-on experience with Trackman, Yakkertech, Synergy, HitTrax, and SensorEdge across three different baseball organizations.

My goal is to work at the intersection of data analytics, in-game strategy and player development - building tools that coaches can actually use and conducting research that translates into on-field decisions.
