My name is Jack Hanaway, and baseball has been a constant throughout my life — as a player, a student of the game, and now as someone who works to understand it through data.

I created this site to organize and share the analytical work I have done across collegiate and professional baseball environments, and to push myself to communicate findings clearly — both in writing and in the tools I build.

I am currently a senior at the University of Tennessee, where I am finishing a Bachelor of Science in Sport Management with a Minor in Statistics. I will be pursuing a Master of Science in Business Analytics beginning in the fall of 2026. My technical work spans R, Python, and SQL, and I have hands-on experience with Trackman, Yakkertech, HitTrax, Blast Motion, and Synergy across three different baseball organizations.

My goal is to work at the intersection of data and player development — building tools that coaches can actually use and conducting research that translates into on-field decisions.
